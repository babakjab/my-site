Three German Quotes inspired by Tractatus

By Babak Jabbar Nezhad · Last updated: 2025-09-17

Drei Aphorismen

I started studying German only a few hours ago. Out of this experiment came not small sentences, but seeds of philosophy.

These three lines grew out of my work with Wittgenstein’s Tractatus in German while thinking in Azerbaijani. Translating in this way is not only vocabulary practice — it lets me see the pattern of thought inside each language. Each aphorism is a small trace of that discovery:

Der Stil der Tatsache ist durch den Gedanken bestimmt.
“The style of the fact is determined by the thought.”
Das sein zu können, was der Fall ist.
“The ability to be what is the case.”
Alles, was durch das Bestehen der Dinge im Bild bestimmt und gebildet ist.
“Everything that is determined and formed through the existence of things in the picture.”
