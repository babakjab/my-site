Unknown

by Babak Jabbar Nezhad

Maybe unknown is a tree in the desert Maybe unnamed is a bush in a swamp Maybe unseen is a yawl in waves Maybe unarmed is bare in the cold Maybe — unwilling is a gate in the unclear

Maybe I see what I don’t feel Maybe I see what I don’t mean Maybe I yell what I don’t call Maybe I yell what I don’t hate Maybe — I will what I don’t want

Maybe, on one side, known comes true Maybe, on harm, named shines through Maybe, at sea, seen sings blue Maybe, on sour, armed has flown Maybe — on will, who knows the clue
